'''Roddy Pontner''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who bet [[Ludovic Bagman|Ludo Bagman]] that the [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team]] would score the first goal at the [[Quidditch World Cup]] in [[1994]]. Ludo gave him good odds because he was convinced that [[Irish National Quidditch team|Ireland]] had the strongest [[Chaser]]s.<ref>{{QTA|B|7}}</ref> He lost this bet and owed Bagman.<ref>{{GOF|B|8}}</ref>
