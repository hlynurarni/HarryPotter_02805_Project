'''Mrs Krum''' (fl. [[1977]]-[[1995]]) was a [[Bulgaria]]n [[Wizardkind|witch]], who was the mother of [[Viktor Krum]]. She was described as having dark hair.<ref name="GOF31">{{GOF|B|31}}</ref>

Mrs Krum travelled to [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] with her [[Viktor Krum's father|husband]] to watch Viktor face the final task of the [[Triwizard Tournament]] in [[1995]].<ref name="GOF31"/>

Her son told [[Harry Potter]] that his [[Viktor Krum's grandfather|grandfather]] was murdered by [[Gellert Grindelwald]].<ref name="DH8">{{DH|B|8}}</ref> It is possible that this man was either Mrs Krum's father or her father-in-law.
