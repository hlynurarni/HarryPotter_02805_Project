'''Mr Krum''' (fl. [[1977]]-[[1995]]) was a [[Bulgarian]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]], who was the father of [[Viktor Krum]]. He was described as having dark hair and a hooked nose like his son.<ref name="GOF31">{{GOF|B|31}}</ref>

Mr Krum travelled to [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] with his [[Viktor Krum's mother|wife]] to watch Viktor face the final task of the [[Triwizard Tournament]] in [[1995]].<ref name="GOF31"/>

Viktor told [[Harry Potter]] that his [[Viktor Krum's grandfather|grandfather]] was killed by [[Gellert Grindelwald]].<ref name="DH8">{{DH|B|8}}</ref> This man could have been either Mr Krum's father or his father-in-law.
