'''Boris the Bewildered''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] from an unknown time period. A statue of him stands on the fifth floor of [[Hogwarts Castle]], near the [[Prefects' bathroom]]. His statue featured a lost-looking wizard with his gloves on the wrong hands.<ref>{{GOF|B|25}}</ref> A portrait of Boris also hangs on [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts']] walls, guarding a secret passageway between the second floor corridor and the [[Third-floor corridor|third floor corridor]]. The password was ''Forget-Me-Never''.<ref>{{OOTP|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Boris died sometime prior to [[1995]], as portraits hung at [[Hogwarts Castle|Hogwarts]] depict deceased individuals.<ref name="DOD">[[J. K. Rowling]] stated at the Edinburgh Book Festival on 15 August 2014 ([http://www.accio-quote.org/articles/2004/0804-ebf.htm source]) that all portraits at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] are of deceased individuals.</ref>

==Etymology==
"Boris" is from the Turkic name ''Bogoris'', perhaps meaning "short" or "wolf".
