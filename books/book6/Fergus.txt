'''Fergus''' (fl. 1979-1996) was an [[Ireland|Irish]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Seamus Finnigan]]'s cousin. He was known to annoy Seamus by [[Apparition|apparating]] around him frequently.<ref name="HBP17">{{HBP|B|17}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Apparition]]''': Fergus was capable of apparating at will, often doing it to simply annoy his cousin [[Seamus Finnigan|Seamus]].<ref name="HBP17"/>

The name "Fergus" means "man of vigour", derived from the Gaelic elements ''fear'' "man" and ''gus'' "vigour". This was the name of several characters in Irish legend including the Ulster hero Fearghas mac RÃ³ich.
