'''Octavius Pepper''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was reported to have disappeared in the early spring of [[1997]] by the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''. His disappearance was believed to be connected to the [[Death Eaters]].<ref>{{HBP|B|21}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Octavius" is a Roman family name meaning "eighth" from Latin octavus. This was the original family name of the emperor Augustus (born Gaius Octavius).
