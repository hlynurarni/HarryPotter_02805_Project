'''Golpalott''' was (possibly) the [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] who formulated [[Golpalott's Third Law]].<ref name="HBP18">{{HBP|B|18}}</ref> Alternatively, it may have been named after him or her by someone else.

==Behind the scenes==
*It is likely that his name is a pun on the phrase "Gulp a lot", which they may have been inclined to do so as a [[potioneer]].
