'''Melinda Bobbin''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] during the [[1990s]]. [[Horace Slughorn]] described her as "charming" and made her a member of the [[Slug Club]] in [[1996]]. Her family owned a large [[Bobbin family's chain of apothecaries|chain of apothecaries]].<ref name="HBP11">{{HBP|B|11}}</ref>

==Etymology==
A "bobbin" is a spool or reel that holds thread or yarn for spinning, weaving, knitting, sewing, or making lace.
