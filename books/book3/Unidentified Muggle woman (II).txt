This unidentified [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] '''woman''' was famous for having spotting [[Sirius Black]] in [[Dufftown]] in [[1993]]. She called the [[police]], but by the time the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] arrived, Black had already gone. She was mentioned in the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''.<ref name="POA7">{{POA|B|7}}</ref>
