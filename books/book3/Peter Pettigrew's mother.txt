'''Mrs Pettigrew''' (fl. [[1981]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and the mother of [[Peter Pettigrew]]. Following her son's alleged death at the hands of [[Sirius Black]] in [[1981]], she was given the [[Order of Merlin]] that was awarded to him posthumously. It is known that the largest piece of [[Peter Pettigrew]] she received was his index finger.<ref name="POA19"/>

==Relationships==
===Peter Pettigrew===
Little is known about the relationship between mother and son. The only thing Mrs Pettigrew received after her son faked his [[death]] the first time was his index finger. She also received his [[Order of Merlin]], First Class, for standing up to [[Sirius Black]] (who was actually framed).<ref name="POA19">{{POA|B|19}}</ref> It's unknown if she lived long enough to find the truth when it was exposed.
