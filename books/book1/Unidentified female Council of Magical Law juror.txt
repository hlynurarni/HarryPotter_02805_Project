This '''individual''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|witch]] and a juror for the [[Council of Magical Law]] in the [[1980s]].<ref name="GOF30"/>

==Biography==
She was present at [[Ludovic Bagman]]'s hearing when he was caught passing information to [[Augustus Rookwood]], who he believed was on the Ministry's side. The witch voted against conviction, as did the rest of the jury, and stood up to congratulate Bagman on his performance playing [[Quidditch]] for [[English National Quidditch team|England]].<ref name="GOF30">{{GOF|B|30}}</ref>
