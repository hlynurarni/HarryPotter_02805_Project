'''Summers''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Hufflepuff]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. In [[1994]], he tried to cross over the [[Age Line]] surrounding the [[Goblet of Fire]] to enter his name in the [[Triwizard Tournament]], despite being underage at the time. He was unsuccessful, and ended up in the [[Hospital Wing]] with a long white beard.<ref>{{GOF|B|16}}</ref>
