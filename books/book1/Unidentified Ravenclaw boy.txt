This '''unidentified boy''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who started attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1997]], who was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]]. Right before the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] on [[1 May]] [[1998]], he prodded [[Alecto Carrow]] in [[Ravenclaw Tower]] while she was [[Stunning Spell|unconscious]], saying hopefully that she might be dead.<ref name="DH30">{{DH|B|30}}</ref>
