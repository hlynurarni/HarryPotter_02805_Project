'''Ptolemy''' was a famous [[Wizardkind|wizard]]. He was featured on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] at some point by [[1989]].<ref>{{HM|?|Scaredy-Cats}}</ref> In [[1991]], his Chocolate Frog Card was one of two that [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] had yet to collect (the other being [[Cornelius Agrippa]]).<ref>{{PS|B|6}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*There are several historical figures who bore the name Ptolemy, the most famous being [[Wikipedia:Claudius Ptolemaeus|Claudius Ptolemaeus]] (c. 90 AD – c. 168 AD), a mathematician, [[Astronomy (class)|astronomer]], geographer, and astrologer who lived in [[Egypt]] while it was under Roman rule. Another famous historic figure was [[Wikipedia:Ptolemy I Soter|Ptolemy I]], who was one of [[Wikipedia:Alexander the Great|Alexander the Great]]'s generals. He was the first Pharaoh of the [[Wikipedia:Ptolemaic dynasty|Ptolemaic dynasty]].
*Despite being mentioned in the first novel, Ptolemy's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] doesn't appear in any of [[Harry Potter video games|''Harry Potter'' games]]. [[Rowan Khanna]] mentioned obtaining his card.<ref>{{HM|2|Scaredy-Cats}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Ptolemy" comes from the Greek name Πτολεμαιος (Ptolemaios), derived from Greek πολεμηιος (polemeios) meaning "aggressive" or "warlike".
