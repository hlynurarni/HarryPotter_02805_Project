This '''girl''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. She shared a dormitory with [[Ginevra Weasley|Ginny Wealsey]].<ref name="OOTP17"/>

==Biography==

===Sorting and early years===
She attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], likely beginning in [[1992]], and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Gryffindor]] [[Hogwarts Houses|House]].

===Fourth year===
In her [[fourth year]] at Hogwarts, she was awoken one morning a day or two after the first [[Hogsmeade weekend trips|trip to Hogsmeade]] of the [[1995-1996 school year]] by [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] attempting to enter the [[Gryffindor Girls' Dormitory]]. However, because the staircase had been bewitched to make a loud noise when a boy tried to climb them, it alerted her and her friend to his presence.<ref name="OOTP17">{{OOTP|B|17}}</ref>
