'''Mrs Patil''' was the mother of [[Padma and Parvati Patil|Parvati and Padma Patil]] and a member of the [[Patil family]]. During the [[1996–1997 school year]], she and her husband, [[Patil twins' father|Mr Patil]], worried that their twin daughters would be in danger at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] since [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] was back. The couple considered withdrawing the girls from the school, but ultimately did not.<ref name="HBP11">{{HBP|B|11}}</ref> Upon [[Draco Malfoy]] succeeding in allowing [[Death Eaters]] entrance into the castle, they immediately reverted their decision and retrieved the twins.<ref name="HBP30">{{HBP|B|30}}</ref>
