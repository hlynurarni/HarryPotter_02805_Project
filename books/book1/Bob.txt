'''Bob''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked for the [[British Ministry of Magic]], under the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]]. He was described as big and bearded.<ref name="OOTP7">{{OOTP|B|7}}</ref>

==Biography==
[[Arthur Weasley]] and [[Harry Potter]] ran into Bob while waiting for a lift in the Ministry lobby on the [[12 August]] [[1995]], the day of Harry's [[Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter|disciplinary]] hearing. He was carrying a large cardboard box that contained a [[Fire-breathing chicken]] that he strongly suspected was a serious violation of the [[Ban on Experimental Breeding]]. When the lift arrived, he got on with Arthur and Harry, and exited at the floor of the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]], the department in which he worked for.<ref name="OOTP7">{{OOTP|B|7}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Magizoology]]''': As an employee for the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]] under the [[British Ministry of Magic]], Bob would have been knowledgeable and experienced in the study of [[magic]]al [[creature]]s, such as with the handling and classification of them. He was able to quickly detect that a [[Fire-breathing chicken]] was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]]-bred creature, showing his knowledge in this field.<ref name="OOTP7"/>
