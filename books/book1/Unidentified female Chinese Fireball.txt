This '''unidentified female Chinese Fireball''' was the [[dragon]] [[Viktor Krum]] had to get past in the [[Triwizard Tournament|First Task]] of the [[1994]] [[Triwizard Tournament]]. Krum used the [[Conjunctivitis Curse]] on the dragon in his attempt to capture the [[golden egg]]. However, in its agony, the Fireball stomped around, squashing half of its real [[Dragon egg|eggs]] and causing Krum to have points deducted by the judges.<ref>{{GOF|B|20}}</ref>
