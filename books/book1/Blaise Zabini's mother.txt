'''Mrs Zabini''' (fl. [[1980]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and the mother of [[Blaise Zabini]]. She was famously beautiful, and married [[Blaise Zabini's mother's seven husbands|seven wizards]] who each died in mysterious circumstances, leaving her with a large amount of gold from each.<ref name="Money">"''Zabini, who was interrogated after McLaggen, turned out to have a famously beautiful witch for a mother (from what Harry could make out, she had been married seven times, each of her husbands dying mysteriously and leaving her mounds of gold).''" ({{HBP}} - Chapter 7)</ref> Her fame was known to [[Horace Slughorn]], who invited her son to his compartment on the [[Hogwarts Express]] for that reason.<ref name="Reason">'''[[Harry Potter]]''': Better reason for inviting someone than because their mother's famous. ({{HBP}} - Chapter 7)</ref>
