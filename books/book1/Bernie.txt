'''Bernie''' (surname possibly '''Pillsworth''') was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="DH13"/>

Under the [[Polyjuice Potion|disguise]] of [[Reginald Cattermole]], [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] was told to fetch Bernie after failing to stop [[Water|rain]] from falling inside [[Yaxley's office]] during the [[Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic|2 September, 1997 Ministry infiltration]].<ref name="DH13">{{DH|B|13}}</ref>
