==History==
In [[1996]] during the [[Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest]], he suggested to the [[Forbidden Forest Centaur colony]] that, after they attacked [[Dolores Umbridge]], they do the same to [[Harry Potter]] and [[Hermione Granger]]. Fortunately, he and the other [[centaur]]s were chased away by [[Grawp]].<ref name="OOTP33">{{OOTP|B|33}}</ref>
