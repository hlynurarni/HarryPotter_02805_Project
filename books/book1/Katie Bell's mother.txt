'''Mrs Bell''' (fl.[[1997]]) was the wife of [[Katie Bell's father|Mr Bell]] and mother of [[Katie Bell|Katie]]. After being discharged from [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries]] in [[1997]], Katie spent a couple of days at home with her parents before returning to [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]].<ref name="HBP24">{{HBP|B|24}}</ref>
