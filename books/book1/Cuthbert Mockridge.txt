'''Cuthbert Mockridge''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]], who was a member of the [[Goblin Liaison Office]], heading the office during the mid-[[1990s]]. He attended the finals of the [[1994]] [[Quidditch World Cup]], and was among the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry]] employees to pass by the [[Weasley family]]'s tent.<ref name="GOF7">{{GOF|B|7}}</ref> He had retired or otherwise left the position by the summer of [[1996]], as [[Dirk Cresswell]] was Head by then.

==Etymology==
The name "Cuthbert" is derived from the Old English (Anglo-Saxon) elements cuæ "famous" and beorht "bright".
