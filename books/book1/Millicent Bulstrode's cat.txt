This '''cat''' was a pet [[cat]] of the [[Slytherin]] student [[Millicent Bulstrode]]. Millicent had it with her as her permitted pet at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] in [[1992]].<ref name="COS12"/>

==Biography==
[[File:Millicents_cat.jpg|thumb|[[Hermione Granger|Hermione]] partially transformed into the cat after accidentally adding the [[Cat hair|cat's hair]] to her [[Polyjuice Potion]]]]
[[Hermione Granger]] plucked a small [[cat hair|hair of the cat]] from Millicent Bulstrode's [[robes]], thinking it was hers. She added the cat's hair to the [[Polyjuice Potion]] she was brewing, which resulted in her partial animal [[Trans-Species Transformation|transformation]] after she drank it.<ref name="COS12">{{COS|B|12}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*In {{LEGO|Y1}}, [[Hermione Granger|Hermione]] did not realise she had become the cat until [[Ronald Weasley|Ron]] and [[Harry Potter|Harry]] pointed it out.<ref>{{LEGO|Y1}}</ref>
