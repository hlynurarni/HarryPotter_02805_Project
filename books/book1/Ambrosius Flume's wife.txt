'''Mrs Flume''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and the co-owner of [[Honeydukes]] alongside her husband [[Ambrosius Flume]].

==Biography==
===Early life===
Mrs Flume was born a witch and possibly attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] as her husband did. At some point in her life, Mrs Flume married [[Ambrosius Flume]].<ref name="HBP4">{{HBP|B|4}}</ref>

===Honeydukes===
By [[1993]], the couple lived in a flat above [[Honeydukes|Honeydukes Sweetshop]] and ran together said shop. In [[1993]], during one of the [[Hogsmeade weekend trips|weekend trips]] to [[Hogsmeade]], Mrs Flume asked [[Ambrosius Flume|her husband]] to get some [[Jelly Slug]]s, as the shop was filled with [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] students.<ref name="PA10">{{POA|B|10}}</ref>

===Battle of Hogwarts===
It is possible that Mrs Flume participated in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] alongside her husband and other residents of [[Hogsmeade]] village, although this is unconfirmed.
