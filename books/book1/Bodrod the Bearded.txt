'''Bodrod the Bearded''' was a [[goblin]] [[Goblin Rebellions|rebel]]. [[Ronald Weasley]] gave his name along with [[Urg the Unclean]] as examples of the kind of names which goblins have, after he was unable to remember all of the goblin rebels' names for his [[fourth year]] [[History of Magic]] final exams and had to make some up.<ref>{{GOF|B|31}}</ref>
