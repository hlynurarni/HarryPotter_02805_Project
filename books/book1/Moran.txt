'''Moran''' was an [[Ireland|Irish]] [[wizardkind|witch]] who played as the [[Chaser]] for the [[Irish National Quidditch team]].<ref name="GOF8"/>

==Biography==
She played alongside [[Connolly]], [[Barry Ryan]], [[Troy]], [[Mullet]], [[Quigley]] and [[Aidan Lynch|Lynch]] during the [[1994]] [[Quidditch World Cup]] final, when they won over the [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team|Bulgarians]]. She, along with her teammates scored one of the points for [[Ireland]], played well, performing moves that astonished [[Harry Potter]] and the [[Weasley family|Weasleys]].<ref name="GOF8">{{GOF|B|8}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The [[wikipedia:Irish_language|Irish]] surname "Moran" means a descendant of Mórán, translated as "big one."

==Behind the scenes==
*Moran appeared in {{GOF|FA}}. It is unknown who played her, and she only makes a small appearance, as most of the match is cut from the film.
