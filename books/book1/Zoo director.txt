The '''zoo director''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who was in charge of the [[Zoo]] in the summer of [[1991]].<ref name="PS2">{{PS|B|2}}</ref> 

==Biography==
On the [[23 June]], [[1991]], when the [[Boa constrictor at the Zoo|boa constrictor]] escaped from the reptile house after the glass in front of its cage [[Underage magic|mysteriously]] [[Vanishment|vanished]], the zoo director made [[Petunia Dursley]] a cup of strong, sweet [[tea]] while he apologised over and over again for the incident.<ref name="PS2">{{PS|B|2}}</ref>
