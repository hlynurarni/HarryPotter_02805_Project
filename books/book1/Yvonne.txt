'''Yvonne''' was a friend of [[Petunia Dursley]]. In [[1991]], she was considered as a replacement babysitter for [[Harry Potter]] when [[Arabella Figg]] broke her leg. Unfortunately for the [[Dursley family|Dursleys]], she was on holiday in [[Majorca]] at the time and they were forced to take Harry to the [[Zoo]] on [[Dudley Dursley's eleventh birthday]].<ref>{{PS|B|2}}</ref>
