'''Eddie Carmichael''' (born c. [[1978]]-[[1979]]) is a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1990]]. He was Sorted into [[Ravenclaw]] house and claimed that he achieved nine "[[Outstanding]]s" in his [[Ordinary Wizarding Level|O.W.L]] exams. He claimed his success was down to [[Baruffio's Brain Elixir]], and attempted to sell it to younger students. [[Hermione Granger]] stopped him and confiscated the potion.<ref>{{OOTP|B|31}}</ref>
