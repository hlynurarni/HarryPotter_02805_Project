The '''owner''' of the [[Railview Hotel]] in [[Cokeworth]] was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] woman. On [[30 July]], [[1991]], she found about one hundred [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] [[Hogwarts acceptance letter|acceptance letters]] for [[Harry Potter]] at the front desk, as the boy was staying in [[Railview Hotel's room 17|room 17]] with the [[Dursley family|Dursleys]]. [[Vernon Dursley]] took these letters from her and checked out of the hotel.<ref name="PS3">{{PS|B|3}}</ref>
