The '''Cresswell brothers''' were the sons of [[Dirk Cresswell]] and his [[Dirk Cresswell's wife|wife]]. In [[1997]], their father was forced to go on the run after [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] [[Fall of the Ministry of Magic|took over]] the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="DH13">{{DH|B|13}}</ref> In [[1998]], he was caught and killed by [[Snatchers]].<ref name="DH22">{{DH|B|22}}</ref> It is unknown what happened to them after [[1998]].
