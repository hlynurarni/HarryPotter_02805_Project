==Biography==

In [[1991]], she was willing to give [[Harry Potter]] some [[Giant Purple Toad wart|warts]] from a [[Giant Purple Toad]] if he could catch it.<ref>{{PS|G}}</ref> In [[1992]], she was absent, possibly on vacation, and a [[Magical Menagerie salesman|man]] had to do her job.<ref>{{COS|G}}</ref> She advised a [[Double-ended-newt wizard|customer]] on how to take care of a [[Double-ended Newt|double-ended newt]], sold [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] a bottle of [[rat tonic]] for [[Peter Pettigrew|Scabbers]] during the summer of [[1993]], and sold [[Crookshanks]] to [[Hermione Granger]] at that time as well.<ref name="PA4">{{POA|B|4}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The Magical Menagerie saleswoman is omitted from {{POA|FA}}, since Hermione already has Crookshanks with her by the time Harry was reunited with her and Ron.
