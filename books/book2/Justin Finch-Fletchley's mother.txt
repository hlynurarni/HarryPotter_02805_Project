'''This woman''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] and the the mother of [[Justin Finch-Fletchley]].<ref name="COS6"/>

==Biography==
She was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]], and was initially a bit disappointed to have her son attend [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], as before he received his [[Hogwarts acceptance letter]] in [[1991]], he had been registered at [[Eton]] College. The [[1992–1993 school year|following year]], Justin made her read [[Gilderoy Lockhart]]'s books, and she came to appreciate how beneficial it might prove to have a fully-trained [[Wizardkind|wizard]] in the family.<ref name="COS6">{{COS|B|6}}</ref>
