}}'''Ladislaw Zamojski''' (pl. ''Władysław Zamojski'') was a [[Poland|Polish]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] who played as a [[Chaser]] for the [[Polish National Quidditch team]]. He was described as the best [[Chaser]] in Poland.<ref>{{OOTP}}</ref>

As of [[2014]], Zamojski did not play for the [[Polish National Quidditch team]] any more.<ref>[http://insider.pottermore.com/2014/03/history-of-quidditch-world-cup-part-two.html "History of the Quidditch World Cup, Part Two to be released tomorrow"] at [http://insider.pottermore.com/ Pottermore Insider]</ref>

==Etymology==
''Ladislaw'' means "famous ruler" in Czech.<ref>[http://baby-names.familyeducation.com/name-meaning/ladislaw Family Education Name Lab]</ref> ''Zamojski'' is a Polish surname meaning "de Zamość".<ref>[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zamoyski_family Wikipedia]</ref>
