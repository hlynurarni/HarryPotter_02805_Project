'''Stubby Boardman''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the lead singer of a musical group called [[The Hobgoblins]].<ref name="OOTP10"/>

==Biography==
He retired in [[1980]] after having a turnip thrown at his ear during a concert in [[Little Norton]] at the [[Little Norton Church Hall|Church Hall]]. He and [[Doris Purkiss]] appeared to be in a relationship.<ref name="OOTP10">{{OOTP|B|10}}</ref>

It was said by ''[[The Quibbler]]'' in [[1995]] that he was in fact [[Sirius Black]] in disguise, based on the word of Purkiss, who claimed that she was having a romantic dinner with him on the night he supposedly [[Massacre in London|murdered]] [[Peter Pettigrew]] and twelve [[Non-magic people|Muggles]].<ref name="OOTP10"/> In [[1996]], ''[[The Quibbler]]'' claimed this person was [[Mina Lima]] in disguise.<ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}} {{see image 2 |File:MinaLimaQuibbler2.jpg|File:MinaLimaQuibbler.jpg}}</ref> Both claims were false.

==Etymology==
A ''boardman'' is a man who perambulates the streets carrying an advertising-board; a sandwich-man.
