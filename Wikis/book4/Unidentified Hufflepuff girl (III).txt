This '''unidentified Hufflepuff girl''' attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] from [[1992]] to [[1999]], and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Hufflepuff]].

==Biography==
===Early life===
This girl was born in approximately [[1981]], to unknown parents. Whether she was born a [[Muggle-born]], [[half-blood]], or [[pure-blood]] is unknown.

===Hogwarts years===
====Early years====
This unidentified girl started [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] in [[1992]]. She was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Hufflepuff]], in the same year as [[Ginevra Weasley|Ginny Weasley]] and [[Luna Lovegood]].

====[[Third year]]====
In this girl's third year, the [[Triwizard Tournament]] was held, as well as, the [[Yule Ball]]. In [[December]], she asked [[Harry Potter]] to the ball. Taken aback, Harry said no so abruptly that he hurt her feelings. He then had to endure the taunts of [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]], [[Seamus Finnigan]], and [[Dean Thomas]].<ref>{{GOF|B|22}}</ref>
