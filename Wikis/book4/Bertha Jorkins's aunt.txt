An '''aunt''' of [[Bertha Jorkins]] lived in [[Albania]].

== Biography ==
Bertha planned to visit her on an Albanian vacation in [[1994]], right after she saw a [[Bertha Jorkins' second cousin|second cousin]], who resided to the north.<ref>{{GOF|B|24}}</ref> However, on the way to her aunt's, Bertha stopped at an inn. She ran into [[Peter Pettigrew]] there, and, after being convinced to take a walk with him, she was kidnapped and taken to [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]], who eventually murdered her.
