This '''Ministry of Magic wizard''' was an employee of the [[British Ministry of Magic]]. In [[1994]], he was working at the [[Quidditch World Cup]], helping to keep up security to prevent drawing attention from [[Non-magic people|Muggle]]s. He was seen attempting to persuade a wizard named [[Archie Aymslowe]] to change out of his flowery nightgown and into a pair of trousers.<ref>{{GOF|B|7}}</ref>
