The '''mother''' of [[Apolline Delacour]] was a [[Veela]]. Her daughter, a [[half-Veela]], married [[Delacour sisters' father|Monsieur Delacour]], a [[Wizardkind|wizard]], and had two daughters; [[Fleur Delacour|Fleur]] and [[Gabrielle Delacour|Gabrielle]]. When it came time for Fleur to have her [[Fleur Delacour's wand|first wand]], she donated one of her [[Veela hair|hairs]] to serve as its [[wand core|core]].<ref name="Wand">{{GOF|B|18}} - '''Garrick Ollivander''': Yes, nine and a half inches... inflexible... rosewood... and containing...dear me... / '''Fleur Delacour''': An 'air from ze 'ead of a Veela. One of my grandmuzzer's.</ref>
