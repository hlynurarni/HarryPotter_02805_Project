'''Florence''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] at the same time as [[Bertha Jorkins]]. Bertha once caught Florence kissing a [[Florence's boyfriend|boy]] behind the [[Hogwarts Castle|Hogwarts greenhouses]].<ref name="GOF30">{{GOF|B|30}}</ref>

==Etymology==
[[Wikipedia:Florence Nightingale|Florence Nightingale]] was a [[Great Britain|British]] nurse who helped heal soldiers in the [[Wikipedia:Crimean War|Crimean War]]. [[Wikipedia:Florence|Florence]] is also the English name for a city in [[Wikipedia:Italy|Italy]].
