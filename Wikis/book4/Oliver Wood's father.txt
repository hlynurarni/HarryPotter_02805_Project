'''Mr Wood''' was the father of [[Oliver Wood]]. He went to the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup]] with his [[Oliver Wood's mother|wife]] and son. While there, Oliver, who had recently left [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]], introduced him to a former schoolmate, [[Harry Potter]].<ref name="GOF7">{{GOF|B|7}}</ref>
