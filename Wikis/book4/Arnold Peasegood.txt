'''Arnold "Arnie" Peasegood''' was a wizard who worked at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]]. By [[1994]], he worked in the capacity of [[Obliviator]] for the [[Accidental Magic Reversal Squad]]<ref name="GF7">{{GOF|B|7}}</ref> and by [[1999]] worked as a [[Hit Wizard]].<ref name="DP">{{DP}}</ref>

==Biography==
===1994 Quidditch World Cup===
Peasegood was one of the Ministry officials stationed at [[Roberts|Mr Roberts]]' [[Mr Roberts's campsite|campsite]] during the final of the four-hundred and twenty-second [[Quidditch World Cup]], in [[1994]].<ref name="GF7" />

===Chipping Clodbury riot===
Peasegood was present during the infamous [[Chipping Clodbury riot]], in [[1999]]. During the riot, he managed to arrest three [[Goblin]]s and suffered a [[Pimple Jinx|minor attack of boils]].<ref name="DP" />

==Etymology==
The name "Arnold" comes from a Germanic name meaning "eagle power", derived from the elements arn "eagle" and wald "power". It was brought by the Normans to England, where it replaced the Old English cognate Earnweald. It died out as an English name after the Middle Ages, but it was revived in the 19th century.
