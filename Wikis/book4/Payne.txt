'''Mr Payne''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who ran one of the campsites close to the place the [[Quidditch World Cup]] was held in [[1994]]. The [[Diggory family]] stayed on Mr Payne's site, while [[Harry Potter]], [[Hermione Granger]], and the [[Weasley family|Weasleys]] stayed on the adjacent campground owned by [[Roberts|Mr Roberts]].<ref>{{GOF|B|7}}</ref>
