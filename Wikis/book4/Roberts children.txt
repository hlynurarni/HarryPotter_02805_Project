These two individuals were the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] '''children''' of [[Roberts|Mr Roberts]] and [[Roberts's wife|Mrs Roberts]].<ref name="GOF9"/>

==Biography==
The two children' of [[Roberts|Mr Roberts]] and [[Roberts's wife|Mrs Roberts]] were attacked by [[Death Eaters]] the day after the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup]]. The youngest, a boy, was spun like a top sixty feet up in the air, while his mother, father, and older sibling floated nearby.<ref name="GOF9">{{GOF|B|9}}</ref>

After the incident, their memories would have been [[Memory Charm|altered]] to forget what had happened along with their parents.<ref name="GOF10">{{GOF|B|10}}</ref>
