'''Mrs Roberts''' was the wife of the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[Roberts|man]] who managed the campground near to where the [[Quidditch World Cup]] was held in [[1994]].

==Biography==
She had [[Roberts children|two children]] with him, including at least one son, who was her youngest child. The family lived together in a stone cottage at the front of the campsite.<ref name="GOF7">{{GOF|B|7}}</ref>

The Roberts family were attacked by [[Death Eaters]] the night after the [[Quidditch]] match. One of the [[Dark Wizard]]s flipped Mrs Roberts upside down in mid-air, making her nightdress fall down, and revealing her "voluminous" drawers to everyone. The other [[Death Eaters]] laughed as she struggled to cover herself. She was not seriously injured during the incident.<ref name="GOF9">{{GOF|B|9}}</ref>

Mrs Roberts' memory was [[Memory Charm|altered]] to forget the incident like her husband.<ref name="GOF10">{{GOF|B|10}}</ref>
