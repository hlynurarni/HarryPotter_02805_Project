'''Wilkes''' was a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] who fought in the [[First Wizarding War]] until their death a year before [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]]'s fall.<ref name="GOF27"/>

==Biography==
Wilkes attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1970s]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin]] house.<ref name="GOF27"/>

According to [[Sirius Black]], Wilkes was a member of a [[gang of Slytherins]] and aspiring [[Death Eaters]] that included [[Severus Snape]], [[Avery (Marauder-era)|Avery]], [[Mulciber (Marauder-era)|Mulciber]], and [[Evan Rosier]]. They all entered the service of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] during the height of the [[First Wizarding War]].<ref name="GOF27"/>

Wilkes fought in several battles of the war. In [[1980]], a year before [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s downfall, Wilkes was caught by [[Auror]]s and was killed, while attempting to avoid being imprisoned in [[Azkaban]].<ref name="GOF27">{{GOF|B|27}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Dark Arts]]: '''Being a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]], Wilkes would have been highly proficient and accomplished in dark magic. More than likely, they would have been able to perform the [[Unforgivable Curses]], along with various other [[dark charm]]s and [[spell]]s. 
*'''[[Duelling]]''': Being a Death Eater who participated and fought in the [[First Wizarding War]], Wilkes likely at least a fairly competent duellist with some skill in [[martial magic]]. Despite this, they were not able to avoid being killed by [[Auror]]s.<ref name="GOF27"/>
