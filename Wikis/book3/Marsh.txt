'''Madam Marsh''' was an elderly [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who seemed to suffer from [[motion sickness]]. She frequently rode on the [[Knight Bus]] nevertheless, suggesting that she travelled often. 

==Biography==
===1993===
In [[1993]], she travelled on the [[Knight Bus]] at the same time as [[Harry Potter]]. She got off the bus at [[Abergavenny]] looking queasy.<ref name="POA3">{{POA|B|3}}</ref>

===1996===
In [[1996]], Madam Marsh was seen travelling again on the [[Knight Bus]]. This time she became sick and vomited. She was let off before Harry, [[Hermione Granger|Hermione]], and the [[Weasley family|Weasleys]].<ref name="OOTP24">{{OOTP|B|24}}</ref>
