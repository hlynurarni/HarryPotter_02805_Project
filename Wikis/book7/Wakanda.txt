'''Wakanda''' {{Fl|[[1997]]}} was an elderly [[Wizardkind|witch]] who worked for the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]], presumably in the [[Department of Magical Law Enforcement]]<ref>As she got off on the same floor as [[Arthur Weasley]].</ref>. She wore her blonde hair in a high style that resembled an anthill.

==Biography==
{{Quote|… I quite understand what you're saying, Wakanda, but I'm afraid I cannot be party to –|[[Arthur Weasley]] to Wakanda|Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows}}

[[Harry Potter]] and [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] encountered Wakanda in a lift when they [[Infiltration of the British Ministry of Magic (1997)|infiltrated the Ministry]] along with [[Hermione Granger]] in [[1997]]. [[Arthur Weasley]] was talking to her, and, judging from his reaction, she had just tried to convince him to become involved in something with which he had reservations.<ref>{{DH}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*The name ''Wakanda'' means "inner magical power" in [[Wikipedia:Sioux|Sioux]]<ref>''The New Age Baby Name Book'' by Sue Browder</ref>. In Sioux tradition, Wakanda or [[Wikipedia:Wakan Tanka|Wakan Tanka]] is also a term for that which is sacred or divine.
