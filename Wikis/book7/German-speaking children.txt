Two young '''German-speaking children''' and their [[German-speaking woman|mother]] were murdered with the [[Killing Curse]] by [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] on [[1 September]], [[1997]].<ref name="DH12">{{DH|B|12}}</ref>

==Biography==
[[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] had come to the family's home looking for the [[wandmaker]] [[Mykew Gregorovitch|Gregorovitch]]. It is unknown if they were related to [[Mykew Gregorovitch|Gregorovitch]] or simply moved into the house after he left. The children and their mother were murdered by Voldemort with the [[Killing Curse]], and [[Harry Potter]] was forced to witness the deaths of the children and their mother due to his connection with Voldemort.<ref name="DH12"/>
