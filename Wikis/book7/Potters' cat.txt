The '''Potters' [[cat]]''' was a pet of the [[Potter family]]. This cat was mentioned in [[Lily J. Potter|Lily Evans]]' [[Lily Evans's letter|letter]] to [[Sirius Black]] that [[Harry Potter|Harry]] had nearly killed it while flying on Sirius' birthday present to Harry, a toy broomstick.<ref name="DH10">{{DH|B|10}}</ref> It is currently unknown if it died when [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] attacked the Potters, or escaped unharmed.
