'''Mr Midgen''' (fl.[[1996]]) was the father of [[Eloise Midgen]]. He pulled his daughter out of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in early [[September]] [[1996]] for fear that her safety there was jeopardised by [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s [[Return of Lord Voldemort|return]].<ref name="HBP11">{{HBP|B|11}}</ref>
