'''Montgomery''' (c. [[1992]] – [[April]] [[1997]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a member of the [[Montgomery family]].

==Biography==
Born in c. [[1992]] to [[Montgomery sisters' mother|a pure-blood or half-blood witch]] somewhere in [[Ireland]] or [[Great Britain]],<ref name="HOME">As his sisters attended Hogwarts and Hogwarts only admits Irish and British students.</ref>, Montgomery had at least [[Montgomery sisters|two sisters]] who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] sometime from [[1990]] to [[1996]].<ref name="SISTERS">As they were still at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] in the [[1996-1997 school year]], they could have been in any year from [[First year|first]] to [[Seventh year|seventh]]; if they were in their first year they'd have begun that year, in [[1996]]; if they were in their seventh they'd have started in [[1990]].</ref>

When he was five years old, his [[Montgomery sisters' mother|mother]] supposedly refused to aid the [[Death Eaters]] in their [[Pure-blood supremacy|cause]] and hence they ordered the [[werewolf]] [[Fenrir Greyback]] to attack the little boy.<ref name="HBP22" /> Although he was taken to [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries]], the [[healer]]s there could not save him and he [[Death|died]].<ref name="HBP22">{{HBP|B|22}}</ref>
