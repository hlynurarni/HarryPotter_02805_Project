'''Rupert "Axebanger" Brookstanton''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]]. He was one of the individuals that [[Hermione Granger]] considered could be the mysterious [[Regulus Black|R.A.B]].<ref>{{HBP|B|30}}</ref>

==Etymology==
"Rupert" is a low German form of ''Robert''. The name was introduced to England in the 17th century by the military commander Prince Rupert of the Rhine, a nephew of Charles I.
