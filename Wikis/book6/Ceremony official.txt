An '''official''' who presided over ceremonies such as weddings and funerals, this [[Wizardkind|wizard]] was described as being small and tufty-haired, with a singsong voice.<ref name="DH8">{{DH|B|8}}</ref>

==History==
In the summer of [[1997]], he presided over the [[Funeral of Albus Dumbledore|funeral]] of [[Albus Dumbledore]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]<ref name="HBP30">{{HBP|B|30}}</ref> and at the [[Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour|wedding]] of [[William Weasley|Bill Weasley]] and [[Fleur Delacour]] at [[the Burrow]].<ref name="DH8" /> Given his occupation, he may be an employee of the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]].
