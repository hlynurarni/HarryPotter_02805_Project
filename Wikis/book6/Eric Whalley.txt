}}'''Eric Whalley''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] orphan, living in [[Wool's Orphanage]], in [[London]], as well as [[Tom Riddle|Tom Marvolo Riddle]]. At the time Tom was eleven, Eric and [[Billy Stubbs]] had the [[chicken pox]] and were being cared for by [[Martha (disambiguation)|Martha]].<ref name="HBP13">{{HBP|B|13}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Eric" is from the Old Norse name Eiríkr, derived from the elements ei, meaning "ever" and ríkr, meaning "ruler".
