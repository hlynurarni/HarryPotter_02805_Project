This [[centaur]] was a member of the [[Forbidden Forest Centaur colony]].<ref name="OOTP33"/>

==Biography==
During the [[Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest]] in [[1996]], this centaur stood at the back of the [[Forbidden Forest Centaur colony|herd of centaurs]] that tried to execute [[Harry Potter]] and [[Hermione Granger]], when he suggested that the two teenagers "pay the consequences". Fortunately, all the [[centaur]]s were chased away by [[Grawp]].<ref name="OOTP33">{{OOTP|B|33}}</ref>
