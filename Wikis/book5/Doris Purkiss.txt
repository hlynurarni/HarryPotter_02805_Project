'''Doris Purkiss''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who lived at [[18 Acanthia Way|number 18]] [[Acanthia Way]], [[Little Norton]]. In [[1995]], she gave an interview to ''[[The Quibbler]]'' claiming that [[Sirius Black]] was actually [[Stubby Boardman]]. She also stated that he had been enjoying a romantic dinner with her on the night of the murders of [[Peter Pettigrew]] and twelve [[Non-magic people|Muggles]].<ref name="OOTP10">{{OOTP|B|10}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Doris" is from the ancient Greek name Δωρις (Doris) which meant "Dorian woman".
