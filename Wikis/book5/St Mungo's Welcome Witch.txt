This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' was responsible for helping visitors and telling patients which floor to go to at [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries]].<ref name="OOTP22"/> 

==Biography==
When [[Harry Potter]] visited [[Arthur Weasley]] with the [[Weasley family|Weasleys]] at [[Christmas]] [[1995]], the Welcome Witch appeared to be a typical secretary type; somewhat rude, brief, and monotonous, which was likely used to be humorous when [[Harry Potter]] read a sign, reading "Our Welcome Witch will be happy to assist you".<ref name="OOTP22"/>

==Physical description==
She was a squat little woman with short, blonde hair and blue eyes.<ref name= "OOTP22">{{OOTP|B|22}}</ref>
