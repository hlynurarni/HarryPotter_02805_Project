'''Warty Harris''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who owned several [[toad]]s that were stolen by [[Will]]. [[Mundungus Fletcher]] stole the toads a second time, then sold them back to Will, who was completely oblivious to the con pulled on him.<ref name="OOTP5">{{OOTP|B|5}}</ref> It is unknown if Warty was ever able to regain his stolen property.
