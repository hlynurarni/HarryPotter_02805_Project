Datasets

Incident Records

The dataset contains the details of every incident responded to by the London Fire Brigade since January 2009. Information is provided for when and where the incident happened and the type of incident dealt with.


Collected from the London Datastore

Source

Mobilisation Records

The dataset contains the details of every fire engine (pumping appliance) sent to an incident by the London Fire Brigade since January 2009. Information is provided for the appliance mobilised, where it was deployed from and the times recorded for arriving at the incident.

Collected from the London Datastore

Source

Postcodes in the UK

The dataset contains geographical information about postcodes in the UK. The dataset is used to enrich the incident dataset.


Originated from The Office for National Statistics (ONS)

Source

Hourly weather data

The dataset contains hourly weather observations from London Heathrow. The dataset is used to enrich the incident dataset and provide context to the weather conditions during the incidents.


Originated from the Met Office and available at the CEDA Archives

Source

House Ages in London

The dataset contains information about the ages of the houses in London. This data is used to enrich the incident dataset and provide attitional information.


Collected from the Consumer Data Research Centre

Source

London Borough Profiles and Atlas

The dataset contains information about different statistics for the London Boroughs, covering dempgraphic, economic, social and enviromental datasets.


Collected from the London Datastore

Source

London Shapefiles

The dataset contains shapefiles for London Boroughs and London Wards. They will used in figures to show the geographical locations of the boroughs and wards.


Collected from the London Datastore

Source
